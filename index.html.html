About Me

Clean code, considered design, real results.

I'm a freelance web developer and website designer based in Kenya, building modern, responsive, high-performing websites for businesses and individuals.

Every project starts with how it needs to work for the people using it — clear navigation, fast load times, and layouts that hold up on any screen size. I care as much about how a site performs on a mid-range phone as how it looks on a large desktop monitor.

Whether it's a brand-new business site, an e‑commerce storefront, or a redesign of something that's fallen behind, I focus on clean, responsive, user-friendly websites built on solid UI/UX principles.

IntFix Home Craft

IntFix Contractors needed a website that could represent a Nairobi home-renovation business — kitchen remodels, bathroom vanities, and bespoke wardrobes — with the same care and precision they bring to their craft. The result is a clean, modern business website with a polished UI and a smooth, responsive experience across every device.

Goal

Give a home-renovation contractor a professional online presence that builds trust with new clients.

Challenge

Presenting a hands-on, craft-driven business in a way that still feels modern and easy to navigate.

Solution

A clear service-led layout with a polished visual identity and fast, mobile-friendly pages.

Result

A live business website ready to showcase services and convert visitors into enquiries.
